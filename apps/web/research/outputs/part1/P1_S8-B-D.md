## Cross-Reference Notes

### Title 47 (Telecommunications)
- Part 64 is the primary TCPA regulation affecting all medical practices that use automated calling/texting for appointments, reminders, or marketing
- Part 54 Universal Service Fund includes specific programs for rural health centers and telehealth infrastructure

### Title 28 (Judicial Administration)
- Parts 35-36 contain core ADA requirements that affect facility design, website accessibility, and patient accommodation
- Part 85 updates civil monetary penalties across multiple healthcare enforcement statutes

### Title 32 (National Defense)
- Part 199 is extensive with 21 relevant sections detailing TRICARE provider requirements
- Particularly important for practices seeing military families and veterans

### Title 38 (Veterans Affairs)
- Contains parallel privacy protections to HIPAA in sections 1.460-1.499
- Community Care Network (17.1500+) increasingly important as VA expands private provider networks

### Title 40 (Environmental Protection)  
- Subpart P (266.500-510) specifically addresses healthcare facility pharmaceutical waste
- Applies to any practice that dispenses, administers, or disposes of pharmaceuticals
