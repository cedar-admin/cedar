### Specific Healthcare Tax Provisions Mapped

**26 CFR Part 1 - Income Tax Healthcare Provisions:**
- Section 199A deductions for medical practices → business-operations.tax-compliance
- Medical expense deductions for HSAs/FSAs → business-operations.financial-policies
- Healthcare professional entity structures → business-operations.corporate-structure
- Tax-exempt healthcare organization rules → business-operations.tax-compliance

**26 CFR Part 31 - Employment Tax Healthcare Provisions:**
- FICA/Medicare tax on healthcare workers → business-operations.employment-law
- Healthcare worker classification (W-2 vs 1099) → business-operations.employment-law
- Locum tenens tax treatment → business-operations.tax-compliance

**26 CFR Part 54 - Health Coverage Excise Tax Provisions:**
- COBRA continuation coverage → business-operations.insurance
- Mental health parity requirements → clinical-operations.scope-of-practice
- No Surprises Act tax penalties → medicare-billing.commercial.no-surprises

- Disease treatment claims → fda-regulation.dietary-supplements

- Breach notification (non-HIPAA) → hipaa-privacy.breach-notification

**Marketing and Communications (Parts 255, 310, 316):**
- Social media endorsements → advertising-marketing.social-media
- Email marketing → advertising-marketing.patient-communications
- Telemarketing/robocalls → advertising-marketing.tcpa-compliance

## Practice Type Summary

**High Relevance for FTC (Title 16):**
- Med Spa/Aesthetic Medicine (advertising claims, testimonials)
- Weight Management (health claims, negative options)
- Functional Medicine (supplement claims, testimonials)
- Compounding Pharmacy (labeling, mail order)
- Telehealth (telemarketing, subscriptions)

**High Relevance for Tax (Title 26):**
- Primary Care/DPC/Concierge (Section 199A, entity structure)
- All practices (employment tax compliance)
